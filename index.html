ABOUT

New or used. Sneakers or shoes. Spiky cap or panama. Brand or rags. It doesn't matter where you got it from, the main thing is that you look cool. You set new standards in the street fashion. Here you are!

We are all tired of the same and repetitive images. Metatrends is a completely different take on fashion. Created by the coolest Dopebroky Daniel, whose images reversed everything upside down not only in ours, but also in the meta-world.
